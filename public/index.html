This is multilink, a bot whose purpose is to redirect you to a set of pages, each opened in their own window.

The limitation of being able to open only in new windows, not tabs is built into browser and is not something that can be done programatically.
But there are browser specific solutions that may work and open the links in new tab(s) not window(s). Look here for them.

By default multilink will only open the links once you press the redirect button but it's possible to open the links automatically.
In both cases you will need to allow multilink.herokuapp.com to open popup windows.
In the future there will be a setting to ignore automatic redirects and always require a button press.

You are using this site on your own responsibility. We cannot guarantee for the content behind any link or it's safety.
